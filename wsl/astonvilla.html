Kenza Dali

Kenza Dali is a midfielder for Aston Villa and France, playing a crucial role for both club and country. In the 2022/2023 season, she was the key playmaker resulting in Villa’s impressive cup runs and 5th place league finish, as well as top assister to Rachel Daly’s golden boot trophy. Her name should sound like “Ken-zah Dah-lee”. Listen to her saying it here or a native speaker speaking it slowly here.
