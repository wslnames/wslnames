Common Mispronounced Names

Guro Reiten

Norwegian and Chelsea winger, Guro Reiten, had a standout season for the Blues, scoring 15 goals and getting 21 assists for club and country and a nomination for the Ballon d'Or. She was the top assister in the WSL for the 2022/2023 season and made the PFA team of the year. Her name is often mispronounced as "Guru" or "Ji-row." It should be pronounced "Gu-ro Reit-en."

Ève Périsset

Johanna Rytting Kaneryd

Johanna Rytting Kaneryd is a critical element in Chelsea's hunt for trophies. Her name is commonly mispronounced and should be pronounced "Yo-Hanna Writ-ing Carn-rid."
