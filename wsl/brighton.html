Common Mispronounced Names

Inessa Kaagman

Brighton & Hove Albion midfielder, Inessa Kaagman, is often mispronounced. Her last name should be pronounced as “Kahg-man” rather than “Kag-man.”

Danielle Carter

Danielle Carter, one of Brighton’s key attackers, is often mispronounced as “Carter” when the emphasis should be placed on the “ar” sound. Her name is pronounced as “Car-ter,” with the emphasis on the first syllable.
